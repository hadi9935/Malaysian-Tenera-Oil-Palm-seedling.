Oil Palm Seedling Growth Velocity

Scenario: Monitoring the growth of a Tenera oil palm seedling over a 24-month period to optimize transplanting schedules. We use a polynomial regression model to approximate height growth.

Mathematical Model

Height Function: \( f(x) = 0.08x^2 + 1.5x + 10 \)

Growth Rate (Derivative): \( f'(x) = 0.16x + 1.5 \)

Where \( x \) = Age in months, \( f(x) \) = Height in cm.
Instruction: Click any point on the graph line to calculate the growth rate at that specific month.

Data points inspired by: MPOB (Malaysian Palm Oil Board) Nursery Management Guidelines and Agronomy Research.
